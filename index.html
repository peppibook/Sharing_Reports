Overview

This is a brief report regarding life expectancy for each country from 1960 to 2015 (source). The data reveals that the country with the longest life expectancy was Hong Kong SAR, China, with a life expectancy of 84.3. That life expectancy was 32.9 years longer than the life expectancy in Central African Republic.

Here are the countries whose life expectancy improved the most since 1960.
